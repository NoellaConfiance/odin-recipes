Chicken

Chicken can be served at lunch time and even at dinner time. It is a more delicious food everyone can prefer to eat in every circumstances. it's preparation vary from one person to another. This is a delightful recipe.

In addition, chicken can be served with different manners. some prefers chicken masala, others chicken peri peri chicken, and baked caesar chicken

Ingredients list

  • peeled garlic
  • Chopped onion
  • Orange juice
  • apple cider vinegar
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Add olive oil in a saucepan
  2. Add chopped onion
  3. Add sliced chicken
  4. Add Orange juice
  5. Add apple cider vinegar
