Beef

Red meat lovers usually prefer beef recipe. It i easier to prepare than any other meat recipes. Also, people like the away beef meat get refrigerated for 24 hours before served. what the sweetest recipe!

In addition, Beef can be served with different manners. some prefers to accompany it with rice, mattooke, Irish potatoes and others prefers only boiled beef meat with ugali.

Ingredients list

  • peeled garlic
  • Chopped onion
  • Maggi
  • Soja sauce
  • red tomatoes

Steps

  1. Add olive oil in a saucepan
  2. Add chopped onion
  3. Add sliced meat
  4. Add red tomatoes
  5. Add soja sauce
  6. Add Maggi
